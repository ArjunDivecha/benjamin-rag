Introduction
Thanks for taking the time to speak with us today. We’re calling from [ORG], a market research company, and conducting a study on nitrogen services (e.g. purging, inerting, blanketing, cooling, pressure-testing, etc.) for downstream oil & gas in the US. We’re speaking with a variety of market participants to better understand market size/trends, customer preferences/purchasing dynamics and how market players differentiate themselves from one another.
